Analyse cinématique de l'épaule chez des patients
  hémiparétiques lors de la saisie d'objets pesants
The aim of this study is to produce a kinematic analysis of movements of the
shoulder complex when hemiparetic patients achieve the grasping of weighted
objects. We propose to describe the influence of the weight on kinematic
characteristics of the gesture and to establish the relevance of several
quantitative indexes concerning the quality of the grasping gesture.
